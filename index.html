About QRPass

QRPass is a powerful platform for managing visitor entry using QR code scanning, payment collection, and automated WhatsApp & Email confirmations.

Features

  • QR-based visitor registration
  • Razorpay integration
  • Automated Email & WhatsApp confirmations
  • Dynamic ticket generation with attachments
  • Retargeting tools

Refund Policy

All payments made via QRPass are final and non-refundable. In case of a failed transaction, the amount (if debited) will be auto-refunded by Razorpay within 5–7 working days.

Terms & Conditions

By using QRPass, users agree to share their contact details for registration and event updates. QRPass reserves the right to update features and services without prior notice.

Privacy Policy

QRPass collects only essential details (name, email, phone number) for registration purposes. This data is not shared with third parties.
